Our Mission

Building confident, empathetic, and responsible members of society

Y-Ultimate uses the sport of Ultimate Frisbee to teach essential life skills to children from under-resourced communities across India.

Through our structured coaching programmes and competitive tournaments, we empower children with values such as teamwork, fairness, and respect. These values help children develop into confident, empathetic individuals who contribute positively to their communities.

At the heart of our approach is the "Spirit of the Game" ‚Äì a unique aspect of Ultimate Frisbee that emphasizes self-officiating, fair play, and mutual respect. This philosophy teaches children to resolve conflicts peacefully, play with integrity, and value sportsmanship above winning.
